Is the Launchfit Club a contract?

No, you are free to come and go as you please. We don't believe in locking you
into any long-term contracts. When you purchase a monthly pre-paid
membership, you have 1 week to cancel for a full refund.

Do you run female only workshops?

No - We foster an inclusive atmosphere where all ages, genders and beliefs are
accepted equally. Training with a diverse group leads to a more holistic fitness
experience for all.




Is there a discount if I refer a friend?

Yes, we offer referral discounts for members on Launchfit Club memberships.
Each friend you refer who takes up a monthly membership entitles you to 1
weeks free club membership.

Tell me more about the meal plans.

Our nutrition specialists have formulated a broad range of healthy meal options
to sustain appropriate energy levels required for our sessions whilst keeping you
looking trim, taut and terrific.
